About

I am a Network/Telecom Engineering Student and a Cloud Enthusiast with a passion for Science, Math, and Technology. My love for these fields began in my childhood through my interest in astronomy and robotics. As I grew older, my passion continued to deepen as I participated in various events, competitions, and hackathons.


Through these experiences, I have acquired a strong set of problem-solving and leadership skills. I am driven to make a positive impact on the world through science and technology, starting with my community in Morocco and Africa. I believe that the advancements in technology and science have the power to change the world for the better and I am determined to be a part of that change.


I am constantly seeking new opportunities to learn and grow as a professional, and I am dedicated to staying at the forefront of technological advancements and scientific discoveries. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and am determined to make a difference.
